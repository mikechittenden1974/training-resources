# EPA Training Resources

These training resources have been created to help you prepare confidently for your **End-Point Assessment (EPA)**.

They explain:

- What to expect during each stage of the EPA
- The evidence you may need to provide
- How to demonstrate your knowledge, skills and behaviours
- How to structure clear and detailed answers
- What is required to achieve a pass or distinction

## How to Use These Resources

Work through each resource carefully and use them alongside the guidance provided by your coach and End-Point Assessment Organisation.

You should return to these resources regularly during your preparation, particularly before completing your:

- Project
- Scenario demonstration
- Presentation
- Professional discussion
- Interview

## What the Resources Include

The resources may include:

- Guidance on the EPA process
- Information about each assessment method
- Examples of possible questions
- Advice on structuring your answers
- Pass and distinction guidance
- Practice activities
- Preparation checklists
- Tips for using workplace examples

## Using Your Own Examples

During your EPA, you should use examples from your own role and experience wherever possible.

A strong answer should explain:

1. **What you did**
2. **How you did it**
3. **Why you chose that approach**
4. **What the outcome was**
5. **What you learned from the experience**

> These resources are designed to support your preparation. Always check the official EPA guidance and follow any instructions provided by your coach or assessment organisation.
